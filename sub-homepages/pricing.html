Frequently asked

Can I switch plans?

Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens after my trial?

Your Pro trial lasts 14 days. After that you'll be asked to choose a plan. No charge without confirmation.

Do instructors need a paid plan?

No. Publishing courses is free. AltiLearn takes a 30% revenue share on sales instead.

Is there a student discount?

Yes — 50% off Pro for verified students. Email edu@altilearn.co with your institution email.

Can I buy individual courses?

Yes. Many courses are available as one-time purchases without a subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and mobile money (MTN, Airtel) in supported regions.
